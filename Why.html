Why 100 Schools?


One student’s voice raised against injustice—though small—is powerful. A group of students each speaking out together is even louder. An entire school full of students voicing their minds is thunderous, and if 100s schools around the world each did their part to stand up and speak out, it would create a roar so loud it would reverberate around the entire globe.

We are students, we are Guria, we are artists, we are speaking out, and we demand FREEDOM NOW for victims of human trafficking.

~


100 is a significant number because it refers to a report released by Guria in 2014 that documents 100 cases when severe police misconduct prevented justice being served to hundreds of victims of human trafficking. We are calling ourselves the 100s Schools for 100 Cases to demand that the world notice the atrocities in these 100 cases published by Guria and to advocate for real action to be taken against the police misconduct that allows trafficking to flourish.

To see what Guria has been up to recently, visit Guria’s blog guriafreedomnow.blogspot.com, Facebook page facebook.com/guriafreedomnow or website guriaindia.org .
